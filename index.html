Who We Are

ShinTwin is a modern marketing agency specialized in Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, and Technology. We create thoughtful written content and develop concrete marketing strategies.

Our methodology is built on four pillars:

The client comes first.

Putting your trust in any business is a big deal. We, especially, do not take that lightly. We'll make sure that we fully understand your needs and will do whatever it takes to meet them.

Professional and personal.

At the end of the day, regardless of where we work or what we do, we're all human. And so, as professional as we are, we'll treat you how you want to be treated, like a person, not a business.

Transparent like glass walls.

We thrive through being transparent. No cutting corners, no loopholes, no bullsh*t. It's no surprise that a company that specializes in communication also values communication.

Going above and beyond.

Why stop when the transaction is settled? We're here to buid long-lasting relationships. This means going above and beyond what's expected to reassure that you've made the right decision.
